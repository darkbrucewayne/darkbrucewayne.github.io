Batoto Scans

A Scan Group by cultured people, for cultured people

Batoto Scans started in December 2020 with the intent of translating high quality smut manga. As the time went on, we picked up more and more projects that our translator wished to do, and expanded our list of works.


If you enjoy our work, please consider donating to us on ko-fi.
All Donations go towards buying raws for our projects.


If you'd like to know more about us and how we do things, check out the Information page.


If you'd like to suggest projects, help out with the scan work, or just have some fun, join our Discord.
